I am currently an associate professor in the school of College of Intelligence and Computing  of Tianjin University .I have obtained my PhD degree in 2009 from Tianjin University . My research interests mainly focus on concurrent program analysis, Deep neural network testing, Self-driving autonomous vehicles' testing and natural language processing.
