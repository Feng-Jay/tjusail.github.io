Profile

I am currently a professor in the school of College of Intelligence and Computing of Tianjin University. I have obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2009 from Tianjin University. My research interests mainly focus on concurrent program analysis, Deep neural network testing, Self-driving autonomous vehicles' testing, and natural language processing.
