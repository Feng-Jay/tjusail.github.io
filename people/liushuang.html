Profile

Shuang Liu is currently an associate professor at the College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University (TJU). She worked at the Singapore Institute of Technology (SiT) as a lecturer before joining TJU. Shuang received her Ph.D. degree from the National University of Singapore and her Bachelor of Honor from Renmin University. Shuang’s research interests include program understanding, anomaly detection, and testing of software and CPS systems. She has published around 20 papers on top-tier international conferences and journals, including TSE, ESEC/FSE, ASE, and FM. She serves as a program committee member of multiple international conferences and reviewers of multiple international journals.
