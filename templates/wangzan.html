I am currently an associate professor in the school of College of Intelligence and Computing  of Tianjin University . I have obtained my PhD degree in March 2019 from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. During my PhD studies, I was honored and fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Shing-Chi CHEUNG in the CASTLE group. I also had an unforgettable visiting experience at UC, Davis under the supervision of Prof. Zhendong Su. My research interests mainly focus on program analysis, automated debugging and software security.
